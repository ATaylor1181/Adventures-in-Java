Character Creation

Races

Human: Nothing special, average across the board

Elf: +2 persuade attack and defence, -2 sneak attack, -2 physical attack

Dwarf: +2 Physical attack and defence, -2 sneak attack, -2 persuade defence

Halfling: +2 Sneak attack and defence, -2 physical attack, -2 persuade defence

Classes

Warrior: +3 Physical attack, -1 sneak attack

Ranger: +1 Physical attack, +1 Sneak attack, +1 Persuade attack

Rogue: +3 Sneak attack, -1 Physical attack

Instructions

Open the console and invoke story().
